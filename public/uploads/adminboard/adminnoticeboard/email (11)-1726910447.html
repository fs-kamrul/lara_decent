Faculty Development Training Session
 
 

On 13 August 2024,Daffodil International University, in collaboration with the Career Development Center, hosted a Faculty Development Training Session today, conducted by the Wadhwani Foundation.
The session, involving 21 faculty members from the Departments of FSIT and FBE, focused on equipping educators with the skills necessary to run pilot programs of the Wadhwani Foundation's 21st Employability Course for students. This training aims to empower faculty to lead this initiative, which is designed to enhance teaching strategies and contribute to the future success of students.

Bridging the Gap: Enhancing Career Pathways and Employment in a Changing Job Market
 

On August 19, 2024, the Deans, Associate Deans, Department Heads of DIU gathered for a significant discussion on the theme "Bridging the Gap: Enhancing Career Pathways and Employment in a Changing Job Market."
The meeting was graced by the presence of Professor Dr. S. M. Mahbub Ul Haque Majumder, Vice Chancellor (In-Charge) of DIU. Alongside with Dr. Mohammed Nadir Bin Ali, Honorable Registrar of DIU & Dr. Mostafa Kamal, Dean, Academic Affairs,DIU.
The discussion focused on addressing the challenges and opportunities in preparing students for successful careers in a rapidly evolving job market.
